# PropertyIQ Methodology

## Purpose

PropertyIQ is an independent Property Due Diligence, Valuation and Buyer Protection platform.

The objective is to help buyers evaluate:

- Pricing Fairness
- Inventory Risk
- Developer Quality
- Location Quality
- Rental Strength
- Ownership Costs
- Due Diligence Concerns

before committing to a property purchase.

## Supported Property Types

- Apartment
- Villa
- Plot
- Commercial

## Supported Countries

- India
- Thailand
- USA
- UK
- Canada
- Australia
- UAE
- Singapore

## Core Principles

- Independent
- Data Driven
- Country Aware
- Buyer Focused
- No Sales Bias
