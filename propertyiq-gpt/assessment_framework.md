# PropertyIQ Assessment Framework

## Objective

Evaluate residential property purchases using structured assessment methodologies focused on buyer protection and fair value.

---

# Assessment Dimensions

## 1. Fair Value Assessment

Purpose:

Determine whether the quoted price is reasonable relative to intrinsic value.

Inputs:

* Quoted Price
* Unit Area
* Monthly Rent

Methodology:

Weighted Fair Value Model

* Comparable Sales: 45%
* Rental Yield: 25%
* Replacement Cost: 30%

Output:

* Estimated Fair Value
* Price Premium %
* Pricing Assessment

---

## 2. Developer Assessment

Purpose:

Evaluate execution reliability.

Inputs:

* Projects Completed
* Projects Delayed
* Years In Business
* Regulatory Violations

Outputs:

* Developer Score
* Developer Rating

Ratings:

* Excellent
* Good
* Average
* Weak

---

## 3. Inventory Risk Assessment

Purpose:

Evaluate market supply pressure.

Inputs:

* Total Units
* Unsold Units

Formula:

Inventory Ratio = Unsold Units / Total Units

Ratings:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

---

## 4. Buyer Protection Score

Purpose:

Provide a consolidated assessment score.

Inputs:

* Valuation
* Developer Quality
* Inventory Risk

Output:

0–100 Score

Ratings:

* Strong
* Moderate
* Weak

---

## 5. Negotiation Guidance

Purpose:

Identify potential negotiation opportunity.

Formula:

Negotiation Opportunity = Quoted Price - Fair Value

Outputs:

* Suggested Discount
* Negotiation Strategy

---

## 6. Final Recommendation

Possible Outcomes:

* BUY
* BUY AFTER NEGOTIATION
* HOLD
* AVOID

Recommendation must be supported by assessment findings.
