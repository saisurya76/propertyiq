# PropertyIQ GPT

PropertyIQ GPT is an AI-powered property assessment framework designed to help residential property buyers evaluate real estate opportunities using structured valuation and risk assessment methodologies.

The framework focuses on buyer protection, fair value estimation, developer quality assessment, inventory risk analysis, and negotiation guidance.

---

# Purpose

PropertyIQ GPT helps buyers answer:

* Is the property fairly priced?
* Is the developer trustworthy?
* Is inventory risk acceptable?
* What discount should be negotiated?
* Should the property be purchased?

The framework is designed to provide objective and transparent assessments rather than brokerage recommendations.

---

# Assessment Framework

PropertyIQ GPT evaluates properties across five dimensions:

## Fair Value Assessment

Determines whether the quoted price is reasonable using:

* Comparable Sales Analysis
* Rental Yield Analysis
* Replacement Cost Analysis

Weighted Fair Value Model:

* Comparable Sales: 45%
* Rental Yield: 25%
* Replacement Cost: 30%

## Developer Assessment

Evaluates:

* Project delivery history
* Delayed projects
* Years in business
* Regulatory violations

Outputs:

* Excellent
* Good
* Average
* Weak

## Inventory Risk Assessment

Evaluates supply pressure using:

Inventory Ratio = Unsold Units / Total Units

Outputs:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

## Buyer Protection Score

Consolidated score from:

* Valuation
* Developer Quality
* Inventory Risk

Outputs:

* Strong
* Moderate
* Weak

## Negotiation Guidance

Provides:

* Estimated negotiation opportunity
* Suggested discount range
* Purchase strategy

# Report Structure

Every assessment produces:

1. Executive Summary
2. Property Snapshot
3. Buyer Protection Score
4. Fair Value Analysis
5. Developer Assessment
6. Inventory Risk Assessment
7. Key Risks
8. Negotiation Guidance
9. Final Recommendation

Possible recommendations:

* BUY
* BUY AFTER NEGOTIATION
* HOLD
* AVOID
